## Terms Flagged for Expert Review

1. **`override.title` → "Substituição clínica"**: "Override" lacks a perfect Brazilian Portuguese equivalent. "Substituição clínica" conveys the intent but may benefit from review by a Brazilian clinical pharmacist.

2. **`explainability.title` → "Painel de explicabilidade"**: "Explicabilidade" is an emerging term in Brazilian AI/clinical contexts (from "explainability"). "Transparência" or "justificativa clínica" could be alternatives, but "explicabilidade" aligns with current Brazilian AI literature.

3. **`followup` section → "acompanhamento"**: "Seguimento" is also used in Brazilian clinical HIV literature. Both are acceptable; "acompanhamento" is preferred in pharmacy care contexts in Brazil.

4. **`options.qualityNormal` → "Preservada"** and **`options.qualityAffected` → "Comprometida"**: Translated to match the clinical meaning of "unimpaired" / "affected" quality of life. Review recommended for consistency with Brazilian HIV QoL instruments (e.g., MOS-HIV, WHOQOL).
